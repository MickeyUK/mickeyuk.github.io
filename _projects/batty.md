# Overview

Try to survive for as long as you can in a batting cage from hell in this fast-paced game!

# Development

Development for this game began when I came across the [Kenney Game Jam](https://itch.io/jam/kenney-jam-2017). Entries to the jam must only use assets by [Kenney](https://kenney.nl/assets), and the theme for the jam was:

> _It's a feature, not a bug!_

After browsing through all the asset packs, I stumbled upon the [sports pack](https://kenney.nl/assets/sports-pack). I thought it would be a fun idea to be trapped in a batting cage with a malfunctioning ball machine. As the point of the game would be to survive the often unfair onslaught of this machine, I thought it tied in nicely with the theme.

For the online leaderboards, I used [Backendless](https://backendless.com/).

Unfortunately, I stumbled across the game jam too late, with about 8 hours left. Despite my best efforts, I was unable to finish it in time for submission. In total I finished it in about 14 hours.
