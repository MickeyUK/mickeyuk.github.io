About Me

Lisa Morris is an Entrepreneur, Philanthropist, Theater Producer, and Impact Investor. A former Broadway actress, she founded the entertainment travel company Road Concierge, which was acquired by the global travel agency ALTOUR. She remains with Road Concierge as President, running the entertainment travel vertical.

After the acquisition, Lisa began investing her own funds into a variety of Impact Investments. She is an Activator with Coralus, a philanthropic venture fund that supports and funds Female Founded companies. She is an Entrepreneur-in-Residence through Columbia with the NYSERDA program as a pitch coach. She is a member of the NEXUS Impact Society, The Unreasonable Collective, Global Leaders Investment Club, Top Tier Impact, and Adoreum Club. She consults for a variety of nonprofit organizations, most recently with the Epic Foundation, Center for Greater Good, and the Mary Tyler Moore Foundation. She sits on the Board of Vital Options international and the WiTT fund Advisory council. She consults for numerous startups. She currently serves as the Director of Philanthropic Services for FORCE Family Office.

Investment Mandate

I invest in social impact companies. Typical stages are seed/Series A, but I have invested in pre-seed companies with particularly compelling thesis. Preference is for female founders or founders of color. I do make some fund investments, but typically only with emerging managers that I know well. My focus is direct investments.

I ONLY invest in companies that have an element of Impact- this can include: theater, education, health/wellness, diversity/inclusion, sustainability, climate, cleantech.

Please do NOT reach out to me if you are any of the following:

  • Hedgefund
  • Crypto/Blockchain/NFT
  • Deeptech
  • Mining/Oil/Gas
  • Social Media
  • Gaming

If you have an early stage social impact company, you may submit your executive summary to: lisa@roadconcierge.com

If I am interested, I will contact you to see a full deck.
